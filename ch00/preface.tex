This thesis has been carried out at the Music Technology Group (MTG) of Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF) and the Multimedia Unit of the Fundació Eurecat -- Centre Tecnològic de Catalunya, in Barcelona, from September 2022 to September 2026. It is the result of an industrial PhD programme in
collaboration between UPF and Eurecat. The work has been supervised by Dr.~Xavier Serra (Universitat Pompeu Fabra) and Dr.~Umut Sayin (Eurecat).

This research as a whole was supported by the Vicente López fellowship, granted by the Direcció Científica of Eurecat, in combination with additional funding from national and european projects. The work presented in Chapter \ref{ch03_hearing} was carried out as part of a collaboration project between Eurecat and Microson (Amplifon Group) and conducted with Martí Baig, PhD student at Microson, and Dr.~Joanna Luberadzka, researcher at Eurecat, and received additional funding from the European Union's Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme under grant agreement No.~101017884 (GuestXR project). The work presented in Chapter \ref{ch04_mbrirs} was also supported by the GuestXR project and conducted with Dr.~Joanna Luberadzka. The work presented in Chapter \ref{ch05_mss} was supported by the Traça IAGen project  at Eurecat. 

Within the context of this thesis, I have also contributed to research efforts from Dr.~Joanna Luberadzka and Martí Baig.
